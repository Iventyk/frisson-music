Frisson Music

Every OST in its rightful place

Frisson Music is a **community-powered platform** designed to structure OSTs from anime, series, movies, and games in a collaborative way.

Our mission is to bridge the gap in organizing media soundtracks, allowing the community to add albums, sort them by seasons or parts, and create structured tracklists that are easy to navigate.

On Frisson Music, users can quickly find their favorite OSTs, explore detailed album information, listen to tracks on Spotify, leave comments, and rate albums based on personal experience.

By embracing a **community-driven approach**, Frisson Music ensures that everyone can contribute to a growing, accurate, and interactive OST database. It’s more than just a library—it’s a hub for fans to connect, discover, and share their passion for media soundtracks.
