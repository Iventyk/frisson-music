Frisson Music Guidelines

Ensuring accurate and reliable OST information

At Frisson Music, all users who add or edit albums are responsible for providing accurate and verified information. Incorrect or misleading entries negatively impact the community and will not be tolerated.

Rules for Filling Album Information:

  • Album Title: Must be the official album name. Double-check spelling, punctuation, and capitalization.
  • Media Title: Enter the correct series, anime, movie, or game name. Avoid abbreviations or fan translations.
  • Media Type: Must be accurate: choose between anime, series, movie, or game. Incorrect media type will cause confusion in sorting and categorization.
  • Part / Season: For movies and games, fill in the Part number. For anime and series, fill in the Season number. If unknown, leave empty but do not invent values.
  • Artists: List all contributing artists exactly as credited in the album.
  • Release Date: Use the official release date (YYYY-MM-DD). Check multiple sources if unsure.
  • Cover Image URL: Provide a valid link to the album cover. Ensure image is publicly accessible and correct.
  • Tracklist: Include all official tracks in order. Avoid fan-made edits or missing tracks.
  • Description: Optional but, if included, must be factual, concise, and neutral.
  • Spotify URL: Optional. Must link to the official album page on Spotify, if available.

Any user found submitting false, misleading, or poorly verified information will face strict consequences:

  • Immediate removal of the incorrect album entry.
  • Temporary suspension or permanent deletion of the user account.
  • Permanent ban from accessing Frisson Music for repeated violations.

By contributing to Frisson Music, you agree to maintain accuracy and integrity. Our community relies on trustworthy data to explore, rate, and enjoy OSTs.
